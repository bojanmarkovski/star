YOUR PERSONAL CONCIERGE WORLDWIDE

Concierge Plus has been offering the best of exclusive private concierge services since its opening in Paris in 2008. Paris was chosen for the company head offices due to its unique location and high quality services it offers. Every year the jet set from all over the world comes to the capital of France to assist to most important international and luxury events.

Concierge Plus is a licenced tour operator and provides bespoke travel packages and concierge services in Europe and around the world 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.Each client is unique for us, which is why we offer special membership programs designed according to your personal needs and for any time. We would be also glad to provide you a one-time service.

Concierge Plus offers various exclusive services, from basic flower deliveries to all parts of the world, tickets to most important events, chauffeured services, to sophisticated tailor-maid travels and events.

Our company has representatives in many countries, and boasts a network of more than 3000 reliable partners. Our staff speaks many foreign languages including English, French, Russian, etc. We would be happy to speak your language with you!
